About Our Team

We are the 9th generation of #12993 from Chancellor State College on the Sunshine Coast, Australia. We are RoboKings Aurum, here to ignite, inspire, and set the world ablaze.

About FTC

FTC, or FIRST Tech Challenge, is a global robotics competition hosted by FIRST for students in grades 7-12. Teams design, build, and program robots to solve challenging, season-based problems. FTC sparks creativity, teamwork, and STEM skills, empowering students to innovate while preparing for careers in technology and engineering. It also fosters community impact, encouraging students to better themselves and their surroundings. FTC provides hands-on experience in STEM and engineering, helping students build meaningful, lifelong skills and connections.
