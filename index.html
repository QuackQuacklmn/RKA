About Us

We are the 9th generation of #12993 from Chancellor State College on the Sunshine Coast, Australia. We are RoboKings Aurum, and we're here to ignite, inspire, and set the world ablaze with innovation.

What is FTC?

FTC, or FIRST Tech Challenge, is a global robotics competition hosted by FIRST for students in grades 7-12. Teams design, build, and program robots to solve season-based challenges, fostering creativity, teamwork, and STEM skills.
